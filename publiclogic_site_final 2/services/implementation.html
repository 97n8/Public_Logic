Implementation services

A clear, step based engagement from first conversation through live VAULT views on PL.org. Built for real towns, not lab towns.

Phase 1 – Discovery and scoping

  • Capture the opportunity in the workspace intake and confirm modules in scope.
  • Short conversations with the people who actually do the work.
  • Align with existing policies, MGL citations, and records retention schedules.
  • Deliver a short Implementation Brief with modules, systems, and owners.

Phase 2 – Structure and intake design

  • Design SharePoint lists and fields for each module in scope.
  • Build universal intake forms on your chosen platform.
  • Define how each form write maps into JSON under the /data folder.
  • Document everything in simple language so staff can see the whole picture.

Phase 3 – Automation and publishing

  • Configure Power Automate flows for intake, publishing, and notifications.
  • Publish JSON files to GitHub or your host and link them to VAULT views.
  • Send clear notifications to staff when new files are created and ready.

Phase 4 – Training and readiness

  • Give staff a gentle walkthrough of the new structure and intake.
  • Offer boards a plain language explanation and a single page to watch.
  • Provide one page SOPs and checklists for new hires.

Phase 5 – Support and iteration

  • Watch how intakes and flows behave in the real world.
  • Adjust metrics and categories without breaking structure.
  • Add new modules as the town is ready.
