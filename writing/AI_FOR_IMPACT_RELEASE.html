PublicLogic and Polimorphic Launch “AI for Impact” Pilot

A people-first pilot that helps Massachusetts municipalities modernize by strengthening structure and institutional capacity

January 20, 2026 — Massachusetts

PublicLogic, in partnership with Polimorphic, today announced the launch of the AI for Impact Pilot, a no-cost, voluntary, twelve-week initiative open to Massachusetts cities and towns.

The pilot begins by stabilizing authority, workflows, and records before applying any automation or AI. The goal is not speed for its own sake, but durability under real-world conditions such as turnover, audit pressure, and constrained staff capacity.

“AI only helps when expectations are clear and systems are connected,” said Parth Shah, CEO of Polimorphic. “This pilot puts structure first so technology strengthens how municipalities already operate.”

“Too often, institutions ask people to carry responsibility the system never clearly held,” said Nathan Boudreau, Founder of PublicLogic. “This work moves that weight into durable structure so staff aren’t carrying it alone.”
