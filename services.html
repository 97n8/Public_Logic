Services

Governance engineering for Massachusetts towns — producing durable artifacts and interfaces that survive without us.

What we deliver

Scoped, law-anchored governance modules, CASE execution systems, and load-bearing artifacts that towns own and operate independently.

Responsibility lives in structure — not in individuals.

Core Services

Governance Module Design & Implementation

Statutory modules (Clerk, Finance, Procurement, Boards, Licensing, Compliance) with authority maps, calendars, SOPs, and continuity rules.

CASE Execution Systems

Bounded execution environments for applications, approvals, renewals, and tracking — governance lives outside the tool.

Governance Artifacts & Documentation

Authority maps, decision records, continuity binders, and transfer procedures — written for successors and auditors.

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

Governance instruments defining authorization, records, deadlines, escalation, and closure — operable by new staff without ambiguity.

Training & Governance Activation

Role-specific activation of adopted structures — focused on authority, escalation, and completion.

Fractional Executive & Stabilization

Time-bound capacity during vacancies or rebuilds — to stabilize while governance is implemented.

Automation & AI Enforcement (Optional)

Rule enforcement only after governance is defined — routing, tracking, logging, drafts — all exportable and independent.

Boards & Committees Governance

Authority clarification, calendars, vote documentation, and risk review — for volunteer-dependent environments.

Our boundary

We do not sell software. We do not offer advisory reports without implementation. We do not create dependency.

The work ends when towns operate the structures confidently, defend them publicly, and pass them to successors without re-learning.
