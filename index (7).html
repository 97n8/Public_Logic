How it works

Lawful and human-centered

Frameworks layer into CivicPlus, Microsoft 365, and Google Workspace. File naming, folder placement, retention dates—all automatic. Staff spend less time guessing and more time serving residents.

Compliance-first, staff-friendly

Plain-language workflows guide staff through each step. Clerk processes route to clerk staff. DPW requests route to DPW. Structure that people can actually manage.

Grant-aligned and measurable

PublicInsight™ tracks adoption, cycle-time reduction, and compliance outcomes. Built to meet funding accountability and reporting requirements for Community Compact, One Stop for Growth, and similar programs.

Framework components

VAULT™ — Verified Automation & Unified Logic for Towns

Automates routing, retention, and audit trails in accordance with Massachusetts law. Forms capture what matters. Rules ensure documents land in the right folders with proper retention dates.

ARCHIEVE™ — Records Logic

Retention and enforcement engine that ensures transparency and compliance. Standardized file naming, folder structures, and retention schedules based on state law make public records requests and audits straightforward.

PublicInsight™ — Applied Research

Behavioral and organizational research framework that measures readiness, learning, and sustained adoption. Tracks cycle-time reduction, staff adoption patterns, and compliance verification to show what works.

Public Instinct™ — Operational Analytics

Dashboard-based reporting on workload trends, bottlenecks, and performance. Shows leaders what's happening without adding reporting burden to staff. Data that protects people, not just processes.
