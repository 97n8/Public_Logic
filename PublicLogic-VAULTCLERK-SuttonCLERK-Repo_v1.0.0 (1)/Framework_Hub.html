Purpose

Provide a secure, repeatable, and transparent municipal operations framework where all SOPs, automations, and retention schedules remain synchronized and municipally owned.

Lifecycle (0–9) & Process Map

Lifecycle Descriptions

  1. 0_EXEC — Start: Launch brief and approvals.
  2. 1_DOCS — Setup guides, smoke test.
  3. 2_PACKAGE — Seed 21-folder VAULT.
  4. 3_CONFIG — Naming, environment.
  5. 4_SAMPLES — Binder demo, datasets.
  6. 5_INTEGRITY — Security, audit, licensing.
  7. 6_AUTOMATION — Power Automate flows.
  8. 7_METADATA — SharePoint columns, JSON schemas.
  9. 8_TRAINING — Playbooks, slide decks, recordings (PublicInsight™).
  10. 9_CONTINUITY — Backups, disaster recovery.

Interactive Folder Map

0_EXEC — Executive Launch

Owners: Town Administrator, Town Clerk

Launch brief, governance approvals, decision log.

1_DOCS — Documentation

Owners: Clerk’s Office

Quick-start guides, smoke tests, runbooks.

2_PACKAGE — Seed Package

Owners: Information Technology

Seeds the 21-folder VAULT structure for reuse and continuity.

3_CONFIG — Configuration

Owners: IT

Naming conventions, environment variables, deployment notes.

4_SAMPLES — Samples & Binders

Owners: Clerk’s Office

Binder demos, example datasets, forms.

5_INTEGRITY — Integrity & Audit

Owners: Town Administrator, IT

Security controls, licensing, audit artifacts, immutable change logs.

6_AUTOMATION — Workflows

Owners: IT, PublicLogic™

Power Automate flows, child-logic modules, routing and notifications.

7_METADATA — Information Model

Owners: IT

SharePoint site columns, content types, JSON schemas.

8_TRAINING — Adoption & Materials

Owners: PublicInsight™, Clerk’s Office

Playbooks, slide decks, recordings, micro-trainings and refresh cycles.

9_CONTINUITY — DR & Backups

Owners: Town Administrator, IT

Backups, disaster recovery, quarterly archive under retention schedule.

  • 201 CMR 17.00
  • ADA Title II
  • WCAG 2.1 AA
