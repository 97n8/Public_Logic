Welcome to Logicville

In Logicville, things just make sense. Requests go where they belong, records are handled with care, and staff can focus on serving the public instead of chasing process.

This demonstration shows how a modern municipal government can operate when responsibility is clearly defined, systems are reliable, and workflows are designed to survive turnover, audits, and real-world pressure.

Governance interface provided by PublicLogic. Authoritative case execution and records management handled by Polimorphic.
