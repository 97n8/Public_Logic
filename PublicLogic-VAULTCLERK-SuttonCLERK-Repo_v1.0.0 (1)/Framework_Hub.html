Purpose

The SuttonCLERK™ Framework Hub is a living SOP for the Town Clerk function. It connects intake, lawful automation, retention, publishing, and training so staff can work a single queue while the system preserves compliance. The Town of Sutton retains all records under M.G.L. c.66 and 201 CMR 17.00.

Vendor-agnostic Town owns data Defense-ready exports Single dashboard

Lifecycle (0–9) & Process Map

Visual process map of the VAULTCLERK™ lifecycle. Begin at 0_EXEC then proceed clockwise through the ten folders. The center ring is the working hub.

Lifecycle Descriptions

  1. 0_EXEC — Launch brief, approvals, quick start.
  2. 1_DOCS — Setup guides and smoke test.
  3. 2_PACKAGE — Seed 21-folder VAULT structure.
  4. 3_CONFIG — Environment, naming, pages.
  5. 4_SAMPLES — Binder demo & datasets.
  6. 5_INTEGRITY — Security, audit, licensing.
  7. 6_AUTOMATION — Power Automate flows and triggers.
  8. 7_METADATA — SharePoint columns & JSON schemas.
  9. 8_TRAINING — Playbooks, decks, videos (PublicInsight™).
  10. 9_CONTINUITY — Backups, disaster recovery, handoff.

Role Lanes

Administrator lane

  • Governance, integrity, and continuity.
  • Use folders 0_EXEC, 1_DOCS, 5_INTEGRITY, 9_CONTINUITY.

Configuration lane

  • Environment & data definitions.
  • Use folders 2_PACKAGE, 3_CONFIG, 7_METADATA.

Automation lane

  • Flows, routing, exports, binder.
  • Use 4_SAMPLES, 6_AUTOMATION.

Training lane

  • Playbooks, decks, recordings (PublicInsight™).
  • Use 8_TRAINING.
