Executive Summary

PublicLogic is system-agnostic. Municipalities own data, configurations, exports, and audit logs. Polimorphic delivers the resident/staff UX and workflow engine; PublicLogic supplies standards, SOPs, and outcome references only — VAULT™ (retention and hold references) and ARCHIVE™ (evidence-package references). Internal mechanics of VAULT/ARCHIVE are out of scope.

Assumptions & Limits

  • Verified facts only; unknowns are marked [TBD] and listed here.
  • Targets and pilot examples (Town, Month YYYY); not guarantees.
  • Time rules: TZ = America/New_York; business days = Mon–Fri; exclude town holidays (configurable).
  • Security posture (targets): TLS ≥1.2; AES-256 at rest; OAuth2/OIDC SSO; SAML2 where required; RBAC; webhook signing (HMAC-SHA256); CSP; HSTS; periodic key rotation; tested backups with defined RPO/RTO.
  • Data portability guaranteed via quarterly exports and on-demand ARCHIVE package requests.
  • VAULT/ARCHIVE referenced by policy/evidence IDs only; no internal logic disclosed.

What Changes for Staff/Residents

Staff

  • Unified queues with SLA timers; policy and evidence IDs surfaced in case pages.
  • One-click export: CSV/JSON/PDF/A-2u with per-file SHA-256 and checksum-of-checksums.
  • Retention and holds applied via VAULT policy reference IDs; no manual date math.
  • Mobile capture (photos/signatures); GIS links; payment receipt IDs attached to cases.

Residents

  • Mobile-first forms; clear status pages with timestamps and receipt IDs.
  • Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA; screen-reader and keyboard tested.
  • Receipts: email confirmations; links to public pages as applicable.

SLA Calculation Example

A request submitted Friday 4:55 PM begins counting Monday 9:00 AM. A 10-business-day clock excludes configured town holidays and ends on the 10th business day at 5:00 PM. Timers pause during formal holds or appeals. Supervisors receive alerts when timers enter a warning state.

Accessibility Gate

  • Automated axe tests for forms, queues, and status pages.
  • Manual keyboard navigation and screen-reader checks.
  • Explicit pass/fail; defects tracked; re-test before go-live.

Holiday clocksBusiness-day calendar with town holiday exclusions; SLA views reflect rules.

Webhook Retry Parameters

Retries: 30s, 2m, 8m, 32m (maximum 4 attempts). Dead Letter Queue alert if more than 5 webhook failures occur per hour.

Naming guidance: case_binder.pdf = {module}-{requestId}-binder.pdf. Attachments may be nested by type; original filenames preserved in metadata.json.
