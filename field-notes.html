What works in real towns.

Practitioner-built patterns from Clerk & DPW rollouts — compliance timers, intake patterns, naming/retention, and safe‑lane automations.

Compliance by construction

  • FOIA 10‑day & 25‑day clocks auto‑tracked; escalation on breach.
  • OML 48‑hour posting guardrails with posting proof preserved.
  • Retention tags applied at creation; immutable audit logs.

Adoption first

  • Start with Clerk or DPW, then expand as staff confidence grows.
  • Use the tools you already own (Microsoft 365, GIS, ERP, CMS).
  • Deliver measurable wins within the first quarter.
